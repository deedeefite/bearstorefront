Our Mission

If you go out in the woods today, you better not go alone. For every bear that ever there was will gather there for certain because. Today's the day the teddy bears have their picnic.

While American black bears rarely attack people, lone, predatory black bears are responsible for most fatal black bear attacks on humans in the United States and Canada, according to a study from 2011.

Stuffed bears do not usually attack humans. But be careful around any of these listed below:

  1. Panda Bear
  2. Grizzly Bear
  3. Polar Bear
  4. Spectacled Bear
  5. Sloth Bear
  6. Brown Bear
  7. Winnie the Pooh
